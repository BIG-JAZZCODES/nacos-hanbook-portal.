ARTICLE 1: PREAMBLE

we the computer students of plateau state university is not being ignorant of the fact that we are saddled with the sole responsibility of the advancement of technology globally, have this conscious need of providing a unique united harmony among ourselves, the university community and the society at large. This is to see the welfare and socials needs of our members. We hereby, do solemnly make and give ourselves this constitution.

ARTICLE 2: NAME

The association shall be refered to as NACOSS meaning National Association of Computer Science Students.

ARTICLE 3: MOTTO

Towards advancement of technology.

ARTICLE 4: AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

  • i. To ease the means of communications to the global community
  • ii. Harmonize and coordinate the activities of all members through out the university community.
  • iii. To give our heartfelt support to the school authority in managing the Information and Communication Technology (ICT).
  • iv. To promote and provide a forum for discussion for local national and international issues through symposia, public lecture, conference, summit and press release.
  • v. To assist deserving individual members who suffer misfortune such as accident, sickness, harzard, etc.
  • vi. To create a conducive forum for the mutual and harmonize cooexistence on matters affecting the welfare and aspiration of the members of the association.
  • vii. To do other things possible and conductive to the attainment of any of the above stated objectives.
